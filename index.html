What We Do

Our Gas & Heating Services

At SP Heating (Sustainable Plumbing & Heating Certified), we provide reliable plumbing, heating, and gas services for both domestic and commercial properties. Our engineers are fully trained and experienced in diagnosing, repairing, and installing a wide range of systems.

Emergency Repairs

When heating or plumbing problems occur unexpectedly, we provide rapid response diagnostics and repairs to get your system back up and running as quickly as possible.

Call Now

Boiler Services

We carry out boiler servicing, diagnostics, and repairs on all major brands including Vaillant, Worcester Bosch, Ideal, and more. Whether your boiler has lost pressure, stopped producing hot water, or requires an annual service, we ensure it operates safely and efficiently.

Learn More

Boiler Installations & Replacements

If your boiler is beyond repair or inefficient, we provide professional boiler installations and upgrades, helping you choose the right system for your home or property. Baxi, Vaillant & Worcester Bosch accredited.

Learn More

Heating Systems

We install, repair, and maintain central heating systems, including radiators, valves, pumps, and pipework to ensure your home stays warm and energy efficient.

Get a Quote

Unvented Hot Water Systems G3 Qualified

Our G3 qualified engineers specialise in the installation, servicing, and repair of unvented hot water cylinders, ensuring safe operation, optimal performance, and full compliance with UK building regulations.

Get a Quote

Leak Detection

Using modern techniques and experience, we help locate hidden leaks in walls, floors, or underground pipework — including thermal imaging and acoustic detection — helping prevent costly property damage.

Call Now

Gas Safety & Certification

As Gas Safe registered engineers, we carry out gas safety checks, landlord certificates (Domestic & Commercial), and safety inspections to ensure all gas appliances are compliant and safe to use.

Get a Quote

Commercial Gas Services

Our experienced engineers provide gas tightness testing, purging, and commissioning of gas pipework, along with installation, servicing, and repairs of commercial boilers and heating equipment, including natural draught boilers and gas-fired tube heaters.

Plumbing & Leak Repairs

From burst pipes and water leaks to faulty taps and pipework repairs, we quickly identify and resolve plumbing issues to prevent damage and restore your system.

Bathroom Installations

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and pricing.

Are you Gas Safe registered?

Yes, all our engineers are fully Gas Safe registered and qualified to carry out domestic and commercial gas work safely and legally.

What areas do you cover?

We cover Surrey and South London, including Croydon, Sutton, Kingston, Epsom, Wandsworth, Merton, Lambeth and surrounding areas.

Do you offer 24-hour emergency callouts?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency callouts for gas leaks, boiler breakdowns and urgent heating issues.

What types of boilers do you repair and install?

We work with most major boiler brands and systems, including combi, system and conventional boilers.

Do you carry out boiler servicing?

Yes, we offer full boiler servicing to keep your system running safely and efficiently and to help prevent breakdowns.

How quickly can you attend an emergency?

Response times vary depending on location and demand, but we always aim to attend emergencies as quickly as possible.

Do you provide free quotes?

Yes, we offer free, no-obligation quotes for all boiler, heating and plumbing work.

Are you insured?

Yes, we are fully insured for all work we carry out for your peace of mind.

Do you work with homeowners and businesses?

Yes, we provide both domestic and commercial gas, heating and plumbing services.

Can you issue Gas Safety Certificates?

Yes, we can carry out gas safety checks and issue Gas Safety Certificates for landlords and homeowners.
